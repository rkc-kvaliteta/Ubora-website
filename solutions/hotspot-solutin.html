Hotspot (VSAT)

With Hotspots, any shop, restaurant, hotel, healthcare centre, school, or even individual can become a connectivity point for the surrounding population.This is our practical and impactful solution to support connectivity deployment in remote areas and actively contribute to their broadband development plans.

Some of the benefits of this service are :

Universal service
The most rural and remote areas in Kenya can be connected.
Complete and ready-to-use kit
All necessary hardware & management software is included.
High speed internet
Hotspots deliver a broadband internet service, with speeds up to 20 Mbps.
Suits every pocket
A variety of plans to meet all budgets; from inexpensive one day vouchers to monthly plans.
Payments made easy
Payment can be made by cash at the hotspot or via mobile/e-payment.
Local knowledge database
Users get to access relevant educational and training content at no charges.
Digital advertising capability
Companies can advertise and sponsor access for the community.

Ubora Hotspot provides access to our powerful, reliable satellite broadband network. The kit contains everything a small business owner needs to run a hotspot while bringing opportunity to the community.
